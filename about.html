About

Braiden K. Arriola is a Statistics and Data Science student at the University of California, Los Angeles, expected to graduate in June 2027.

His work sits at the intersection of data analytics, product management, and predictive modeling — applying statistical rigor to real business problems and translating findings into clear narratives for stakeholders.

At BeReal, he mapped user journeys across 20M+ users and authored product requirement documents grounded in evidence. At Beats By Dre, he built consumer insight dashboards and delivered executive strategy reports that aligned cross-functional teams.

His analytical projects include a Kaggle Housing Price Prediction model that placed 6th out of 69 teams with 93% accuracy, and a Billboard Top 100 analysis identifying musical trends across two decades of chart data.

Based in West Hills, California. Open to internships and collaborative work.
